CLASSIFIED LEVEL 3

United Security Federation

Data Protection & Metadata Custody

Document Ref: USF-PRIV-2026-X

1. Information Collection Protocol

In accordance with the Anti-Raid Directive, the USF Division collects technical metadata during the handshake process. This includes Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, regional geolocations, browser user-agents, and temporary session cryptographic keys.

2. Purpose of Processing

Data is processed strictly for the identification of hostile actors and the mitigation of automated network attacks. Collection is required to validate the human status of the Subject and ensure compliance with Federation security guidelines.

3. Data Retention & Custody

Metadata is retained within the secure USF Central Intelligence Database. Records for verified non-hostile subjects are periodically purged, while records associated with identified security threats are archived indefinitely for intelligence purposes.

4. Subject Rights

Due to the restricted nature of the security protocols, disclosure of specific data points or the deletion of intelligence records is subject to approval by the USF Intelligence Division Oversight Committee.
